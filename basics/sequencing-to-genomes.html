🧫 从测序到基因组From sequencing to genomes

给非生物背景读者:一份基因组是怎么从"一管样本"变出来的——装配、测序数据档案(SRA/SRR)、宏基因组分箱,以及 NCBI 装配号和那个分片 FTP 目录怎么读。For non-biologists: how a genome comes to exist from "a tube of sample" — assembly, sequence archives (SRA/SRR), metagenomic binning, and how to read an NCBI assembly accession and its sharded FTP directory.
TL;DR: 一条主线:样本 → 测序(得到一堆乱序碎片 reads,存进 SRA,编号如 SRR…)→ 装配(把碎片拼成基因组)→ 登记装配号(GCA/GCF…)。如果样本是"一锅多物种混合"(宏基因组),还要分箱把混杂序列按物种归类,挖出一个个 MAG。分箱靠两个信号:序列的 k-mer 指纹 和 覆盖深度(丰度)。NCBI 把每份装配放在按装配号切三段的 FTP 目录里,目录里的文件分三类:数据本身(fna/gbff)、说明书(assembly_report/stats)、质检报告(ani/fcs)。 One backbone: sample → sequencing (a pile of shuffled fragments = reads, stored in the SRA with an ID like SRR…) → assembly (piece the fragments into a genome) → register an assembly accession (GCA/GCF…). If the sample is a "mixed pot of many organisms" (a metagenome), you additionally bin the jumbled sequences by organism to recover individual MAGs. Binning uses two signals: each sequence's k-mer fingerprint and its coverage depth (abundance). NCBI stores each assembly in an FTP directory sharded from the accession into three digit-groups; its files fall into three kinds: the data itself (fna/gbff), the "manuals" (assembly_report/stats), and the "QC reports" (ani/fcs).

2装配 (assembly):把碎片拼回基因组Assembly: piecing fragments back into a genome

装配既是动作也是产物:把测序得到的一堆 DNA 碎片,拼回成完整(或尽量完整)的基因组——拼这个动作叫装配,拼出来的成品也叫"一份装配"。Assembly is both a verb and a noun: piecing the pile of sequenced DNA fragments back into a complete (or as-complete-as-possible) genome — the act is "assembly", and the product is also called "an assembly".

为什么需要它Why it's needed

测序仪读不完整条染色体(人 1 号染色体有 2.5 亿碱基),只能把 DNA 打断成几百~几万碱基的小片段逐条读——一次产出几千万~几亿条乱序、彼此重叠的碎片(reads)。要得到完整基因组,就得把它们拼回去。A sequencer can't read a whole chromosome (human chr1 has 250 million bases); it breaks DNA into fragments of hundreds-to-tens-of-thousands of bases and reads them one by one — tens of millions to billions of shuffled, overlapping fragments (reads) per run. To get a complete genome you must reassemble them.

🧩 想象把一本书撕成几百万张小纸片(每张几个词)再打乱。装配 = 靠纸片间重叠的词判断谁接谁,一点点拼回整本书。拼出来的"整本书"就是一份基因组装配。🧩 Imagine shredding a book into millions of scraps (a few words each) and shuffling them. Assembly = using the overlapping words between scraps to work out what follows what, rebuilding the whole book. The rebuilt "book" is a genome assembly.

产物与质量Product & quality

拼的结果是一批尽量长的连续序列(contig 连续片段,进一步排序定向连成 scaffold)。理想能拼成完整染色体,但常常是草图。衡量质量常看 N50(把 contig 按长度排,累计到一半总长时那条 contig 的长度——越大说明拼得越连续)、总长、contig 数、GC%。这些就在装配目录的 *_assembly_stats.txt 里(见第 8 节)。The output is a set of long-as-possible continuous sequences (contigs; further ordered/oriented into scaffolds). Ideally whole chromosomes, but often a draft. Quality is judged by N50 (sort contigs by length; the length of the contig at which cumulative length reaches half the total — bigger = more contiguous), total length, contig count, GC%. These live in *_assembly_stats.txt in the assembly directory (see §8).

装配 ≠ 注释:装配是把序列拼出来(得到"字母 + 坐标系");注释是在拼好的序列上标哪段是基因(GENCODE/Ensembl 干的,见另一页)。先有装配,才能注释。装配还有"版本",如 GRCh38 = 人类参考基因组第 38 版装配。Assembly ≠ annotation: assembly builds the sequence (giving letters + a coordinate system); annotation labels which stretch is a gene on top (GENCODE/Ensembl, other page). Assembly comes first. Assemblies are versioned too, e.g. GRCh38 = human reference build 38.

3SRA:原始测序数据的档案库SRA: the archive of raw sequencing data

SRA = Sequence Read Archive(测序读长档案库),NCBI 的公共数据库,专存原始 reads(测序仪吐出来、还没拼没分析的生料)。全球研究者把原始数据传上去公开,别人可下载复现。SRA = Sequence Read Archive, NCBI's public database for raw reads — the unassembled, unanalyzed output straight from the sequencer. Researchers deposit raw data publicly so others can download and reproduce.

"run" 与编号体系"Run" & the accession hierarchy

run(运行)= 测序仪对一个文库跑一次测序的产物,一次 run 出一批 reads,是 SRA 里最小的数据单元。像 SRR30861082 就是某一次 run 的编号(SRR 前缀 + 数字),凭它能唯一定位那批原始 reads。SRA 的编号是分层的:A run = the product of one sequencing execution on one library; each run yields a batch of reads and is the smallest data unit in the SRA. Something like SRR30861082 is one run's accession (SRR prefix + digits) that uniquely locates that batch of raw reads. SRA accessions are layered:

SRR 是最底层——具体的数据本身,也是你在装配名里最常见到的(标明"这基因组的原料是哪批 reads",可溯源)。SRR is the bottom layer — the data itself, and the one you most often see inside an assembly name (marking "which reads this genome was built from", for provenance).

5宏基因组与分箱:从"一锅混样"挖基因组Metagenomes & binning: mining genomes from a mixed pot

普通测序:先纯培养一种微生物再单独测 → 一个干净基因组。但大多数微生物没法培养,且天然是成千上万种混在一起(肠道/土壤/海水)。宏基因组学=不分离,直接把样本里所有微生物的 DNA 一锅全测,产物是来自几百上千物种、混在一起的 reads。Normal sequencing: pure-culture one microbe, sequence it alone → one clean genome. But most microbes can't be cultured, and are naturally thousands of species mixed together (gut/soil/seawater). Metagenomics = don't separate; sequence all the DNA in the sample at once, yielding reads from hundreds-to-thousands of species jumbled together.

🎙️ 不是录一个人独唱,而是把整个嘈杂菜市场的声音一次性全录下来。🎙️ Not recording one soloist, but recording the whole noisy marketplace all at once.

把这坨混合 reads 装配后,得到一堆 contig——但它们属于不同微生物、全混着、没标签。分箱(binning)就是:把这些 contig 按"来自同一个微生物"重新归类分组,每组(一个"箱")≈ 一个微生物的基因组草图。Assembling that mixed pile gives a heap of contigs — but they come from different microbes, all mixed, unlabeled. Binning means regrouping those contigs by "which microbe they came from"; each group (a "bin") ≈ one microbe's draft genome.

一个箱拼出的基因组叫 MAG(Metagenome-Assembled Genome,宏基因组组装基因组)——从环境样本里"挖"出来、而非纯培养得到的微生物基因组(通常是草图,可能不完整/有杂质,所以要看质检报告,见第 8 节)。A genome from one bin is a MAG (Metagenome-Assembled Genome) — a microbial genome "mined" from an environmental sample rather than pure-cultured (usually a draft, possibly incomplete/impure — hence the QC reports in §8).

6分箱靠什么信号:k-mer 指纹 + 覆盖深度What binning uses: k-mer fingerprint + coverage depth

① 碱基组成 / k-mer 频率(序列指纹)① Base composition / k-mer frequency (a sequence fingerprint)

碱基组成:一段序列里 A/C/G/T 的比例和模式,最简单的是 GC 含量(G+C 占比),不同物种常常不同。k-mer = 长度为 k 的连续小片段;比如 4-mer(四联体)是所有长度 4 的"词"(ACGT、TTGC…共 4⁴=256 种)。k-mer 频率 = 每种词出现的频次分布。关键:同一基因组的不同片段,k-mer 频率高度相似——像"同一作者的文风/指纹"。分箱就把 k-mer 指纹相近的 contig 归一起(四联体频率是经典特征)。Base composition: the proportions/patterns of A/C/G/T in a sequence; the simplest is GC content (fraction of G+C), which often differs between species. A k-mer is a continuous substring of length k; e.g. a 4-mer (tetranucleotide) is any length-4 "word" (ACGT, TTGC, … 4⁴=256 of them). k-mer frequency = the distribution of how often each word appears. Key: different fragments of the same genome share very similar k-mer frequencies — like one author's writing style / fingerprint. Binning groups contigs with similar k-mer fingerprints (tetranucleotide frequency is the classic feature).

"从哪个碱基起始"不影响 k-mer 计数:数 k-mer 用的是滑动窗口、步长 1——每个位置都取一个,全部重叠地数(ACGT / CGTA / GTAC…),所以不存在"起始位不同"的歧义。这和密码子不同:密码子才有阅读框、步长 3、要区分起始。两个经典处理:① 反向互补合并(canonical)——双链 DNA 不知道 contig 来自哪条链,把一个 4-mer 和它的反向互补当同一个数,256 种 → 136 种;② 经典特征就是这套四联体频率(TNF)(z-score 标准化的 TETRA,MetaBAT2 / CONCOCT / MaxBin2 都在用)。The "which base to start from" worry doesn't apply to k-mer counting: it uses a sliding window, step 1 — every position yields one k-mer, all overlapping (ACGT / CGTA / GTAC…), so there is no "start-position" ambiguity. This differs from codons, which do have a reading frame (step 3, start matters). Two classic touches: (i) reverse-complement canonicalization — double-stranded DNA means you don't know which strand a contig came from, so a 4-mer and its reverse complement are counted as one, 256 → 136; (ii) the classic feature is exactly this tetranucleotide frequency (TNF) (z-score-normalized TETRA; used by MetaBAT2 / CONCOCT / MaxBin2).

② 覆盖深度 / 丰度② Coverage depth / abundance

覆盖深度:某位置平均被多少条 reads 覆盖(如 50× = 每个位置平均 50 条 reads 经过)。在宏基因组里,每个微生物有个丰度(细胞越多→DNA 越多→贡献的 reads 越多→它的 contig 覆盖深度越高;稀有的就低)。Coverage depth: how many reads cover a given position on average (e.g. 50× = ~50 reads pass through each position). In a metagenome each microbe has an abundance (more cells → more DNA → more reads → higher coverage on its contigs; rare ones → lower).

片段被打散了,怎么算覆盖?靠"比对回 contig",不是靠"找相同片段":装配出 contig(重建的连续序列)后,把原始 reads 拿去和 contig 做序列比对,数每个位置压了多少条 read = 覆盖深度。你担心的"有的从 3 号截断、有的从 13 号截断"其实不影响——比对天然消化错位:一条从 pos 3 起、一条从 pos 13 起的 read,都能各自对齐到同一条 contig 的不同区段,各贡献自己那段的覆盖。所以"相同"指的不是"片段一样",而是"能对齐到同一条重建序列"。另外,分箱恢复的是物种/种群级基因组(不是字面某一个细胞):该物种的所有细胞都贡献 reads 到同一批 contig,"丰度"= 这个种群的整体丰度。How can broken fragments give a coverage number? By mapping reads back to contigs, not by matching identical fragments: once a contig (reconstructed continuous sequence) is assembled, the raw reads are aligned to it, and coverage depth = how many reads sit over each position. The worry that "some are cut at position 3, some at 13" doesn't matter — alignment absorbs the offset: a read starting at pos 3 and one starting at pos 13 both align to different spans of the same contig, each contributing coverage to its own span. So "the same" means "aligns to the same reconstructed sequence", not "identical fragment". Also, binning recovers a species/population-level genome (not literally one cell): all cells of that species contribute reads to the same contigs, and "abundance" is that population's collective abundance.

为什么能分箱:同一个微生物的所有 contig,丰度相同 → 覆盖深度应当一致。所以"深度相近"是个按物种的信号——深度相近的 contig 很可能是一伙的。Why it bins: all contigs from the same microbe share one abundance → their coverage depths should match. So "similar depth" is a per-organism signal — contigs with similar depth likely belong together.

📈 差异丰度分箱(更强):若有多个样本,同一微生物在各样本里的丰度会同涨同落;那些"跨样本变化曲线一致"的 contig 就是一伙的——比单样本分得更准。
把 k-mer 指纹(①)+ 覆盖深度(②) 结合,分箱工具就能把混杂 contig 归成一个个 MAG。📈 Differential-coverage binning (stronger): with multiple samples, a microbe's abundance rises and falls together across samples; contigs whose across-sample curves match belong together — more accurate than a single sample.
Combine the k-mer fingerprint (①) + coverage depth (②), and binning tools group jumbled contigs into individual MAGs.

那几个数字不是随机的——是装配号数字部分 981441385 每 3 位切一段:981 / 441 / 385。为什么切?NCBI 有几百万个装配,全塞一个目录会让文件系统/网页列表崩溃;切成三级树,每层最多 1000 个子目录,查找快。版本号 .1 不进分片路径,只出现在最后的文件夹名里。Those digits aren't random — they're the numeric part 981441385 split every 3 digits: 981 / 441 / 385.
