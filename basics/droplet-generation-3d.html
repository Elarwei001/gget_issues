流动聚焦(flow-focusing):中央水相带着细胞(粉)和微珠(深)向右流,在交叉口被上下两股油(琥珀)夹紧、剪断,一个个大小一致的液滴连续生成、向右流走。目标是每滴 1 细胞 + 1 珠;但多数液滴按泊松分布只有珠、没细胞(正常损耗)。 Flow-focusing: the central aqueous stream carries cells (pink) and beads (dark) to the right; at the junction two oil streams (amber) from top & bottom squeeze and pinch it into uniform droplets that flow off. The goal is 1 cell + 1 bead per droplet, but by Poisson most droplets have a bead only, no cell (expected loss).
